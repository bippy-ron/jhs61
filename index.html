    60th Reunion Events

    Our classmates have scattered all over the country and even to Canada and Columbia! While a few are in the Jerome area, many more are in other parts of Idaho such as the areas near Boise, Twin Falls, and Sun Valley. Utah and Arizona have become popular places of residence. We have also scattered to both the West and East coasts. The world is a better place because of the influence of JHS61 classmates!
